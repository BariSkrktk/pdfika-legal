Delete Your PDFika Account

PDFika by BarisTechApps

You can delete your PDFika account and all associated data directly from within the app. Follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the PDFika app on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar).
  3. Tap Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete My Account.
  5. Enter your current password to verify your identity.
  6. Tap Delete Account to confirm.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you have saved any important files to your device before proceeding.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile (name, email address)
  • Your premium subscription record
  • Any cloud-synced preferences

What Is Not Affected

  • Files stored locally on your device — these remain until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Files you have already saved to your device's storage

Data Retention After Deletion

Upon account deletion, your personal information (name and email) is immediately removed from our systems. No additional retention period applies to personal data.

Anonymised usage records (if any) may be retained for up to 90 days for security and fraud prevention purposes, after which they are permanently deleted.
